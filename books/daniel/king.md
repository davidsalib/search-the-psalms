[**Daniel 11:13**](../bible/daniel/daniel-11.md) — For the **king** of the North will return and muster a multitude greater than the former, and shall certainly come at the end of some years with a great army and much equipment.

[**Daniel 11:14**](../bible/daniel/daniel-11.md) — “Now in those times many shall rise up against the **king** of the South. Also, violent men of your people shall exalt themselves in fulfillment of the vision, but they shall fall.

[**Daniel 11:15**](../bible/daniel/daniel-11.md) — So the **king** of the North shall come and build a siege mound, and take a fortified city; and the forces of the South shall not withstand him. Even his choice troops shall have no strength to resist.

[**Daniel 11:25**](../bible/daniel/daniel-11.md) — “He shall stir up his power and his courage against the **king** of the South with a great army. And the **king** of the South shall be stirred up to battle with a very great and mighty army; but he shall not stand, for they shall devise plans against him.

[**Daniel 11:36**](../bible/daniel/daniel-11.md) — “Then the **king** shall do according to his own will: he shall exalt and magnify himself above every god, shall speak blasphemies against the God of gods, and shall prosper till the wrath has been accomplished; for what has been determined shall be done.

[**Daniel 11:40**](../bible/daniel/daniel-11.md) — “At the time of the end the **king** of the South shall attack him; and the **king** of the North shall come against him like a whirlwind, with chariots, horsemen, and with many ships; and he shall enter the countries, overwhelm them, and pass through.
