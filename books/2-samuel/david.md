[**2 Samuel 24:24**](../bible/2-samuel/2-samuel-24.md) — Then the king said to Araunah, “No, but I will surely buy it from you for a price; nor will I offer burnt offerings to the Lord my God with that which costs me nothing.” So **David** bought the threshing floor and the oxen for fifty shekels of silver.

[**2 Samuel 24:25**](../bible/2-samuel/2-samuel-24.md) — And **David** built there an altar to the Lord, and offered burnt offerings and peace offerings. So the Lord heeded the prayers for the land, and the plague was withdrawn from Israel.
